The 2021 Top 10 Snapshot Collection is a collection of cards created to chronicle the top 10 crypto cards of 2021. It was released on FTM via a ZooCoin collection, and on Polygon via Opensea.

The grey and yellow background are actually Pantone's colours of the year.
